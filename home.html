About Our Company

To ensure students continue to learn even when they can't come to school, teachers need effective resources that can be accessed even with basic devices and data constraints.


CAPE brings to you tried and tested lesson-plans, worksheets, videos, and teaching guides to help make this happen. They are sorted by board, subject, and grade so that everything is in an organized and accessible format. CAPE is here to bring teachers the tools they need to be online teaching superheroes!
